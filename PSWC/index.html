A Little About Puget Sound Window Cleaners

Our business is part of a remarkable 17-year journey in the window cleaning industry. We are licensed, bonded and insured and are highly concerned with making sure our reputation reflects five-star quality work for each client. Armed with a passion for pristine views and a keen eye for detail, we have honed cleaning skills to perfection over the years.

Our Services

Window Cleaning

We offer two techniques for cleaning windows: traditional squeegee and applicator or a purified water fed pole system for hard-to-reach areas. Screen and track cleaning are also available for an additional cost.

Gutter Cleaning

Our gutter cleaning service ensures thorough cleaning of the gutter interior, enhancing functionality and aesthetics for a well-maintained home or business.

Pressure Washing

We effectively remove moss and debris from concrete, block paving, and tarmac. Our pressure washing service also covers decks and outdoor areas.

Roof Cleaning and Moss Removal

Our roof cleaning service eliminates moss and prevents future growth. We use a specialized solution that protects your roof while enhancing its appearance.

External Cleaning & House Washing

Our external cleaning services are tailored to meet the unique needs of your home, ensuring optimal results and maintaining your home's exterior.

Home Cleaning Services

Our home cleaning services provide a thorough and detailed clean for your living spaces, ensuring a fresh and inviting environment for you and your family.
